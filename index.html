My Recent Work

A few of my recent projects:

Entertain.Me

Finding local events is easy with entertain.me. Food, music, family events, and more display at the click of a button. Target your searches by activity, location, and/or travel distance. Let's do something!

Wishful Reading

Track the books you have always wanted to read with the Wishful Reading app. Add and edit your current list, and delete books as you are finished reading them. Can also be used to track books that you have already completed so you don't rebuy or reread.

Moodsense - Emotional Awareness Tracker

Your emotional health is important! Moodsense is a simple app allowing you to select your current mood, rate the intensity of those feelings, and log an entry for later review. Information can be shared with family, medical professionals, or kept private. Record your mood whenever you choose.
